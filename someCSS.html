Drake is this generation's GOAT



Drake has been performing hits for the past 12 years. Since his days in 2006 when he dropped his mixtape So Far Gone, which had great songs including "Best I Ever Had" and "Successful," he has been a star. Drake gained popularity from not only this mixtape, but also his time on Degrassi: The Next Generation. Drake has been killing the game forever, and it seems like he has no intentions of slowing down anytime soon.



The Drake appeal



On every track Drake hops on, you know it's going to be good. Whether he is rapping like in "Weston Road Flows" or singing like in "Fire & Desire," it's going to make you feel either very happy or in your feelings. That's another great thing about Drake, he has a pretty big range. When it comes to his music he can go from talking about gangs such as in "100" ft. The Game, or he can come up with a song like "Marvin's Room" where you are left thinking about your ex. Drake overall has a great voice and with his witty lines, and relatable stories, it makes him one of the greatest artists of our generation.
